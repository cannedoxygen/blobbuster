## üìù PRACTICE DRILL

Read these pitches out loud 10 times each:

1. 10-second pitch (memorize perfectly)
2. 30-second pitch (memorize perfectly)
3. 2-minute pitch (know structure, improvise details)

**Timer yourself:**
- 10-sec pitch should be 8-12 seconds
- 30-sec pitch should be 25-35 seconds
- 2-min pitch should be 1:45-2:15

**Record yourself:**
- Use your phone
- Watch for filler words ("um", "like", "you know")
- Check energy level (are you excited?)

**Practice on friends:**
- Non-crypto friends (test simplicity)
- Crypto friends (test technical depth)
- Investor friends (test conviction)

## üé¨ DELIVERY TIPS

### Energy Level
- **High energy!** This is a fun, nostalgic brand
- Smile when you say "Blockbuster is back"
- Pause for impact after "70%"

### Body Language
- Make eye contact
- Open gestures (not crossed arms)
- Lean forward slightly (shows eagerness)

### Vocal Variety
- **Emphasize**: "70%", "$5/month", "2-3 seconds"
- **Slow down**: When stating the ask ($50k)
- **Speed up**: When talking about traction (shows momentum)

### Handling Nerves
- Take a deep breath before starting
- Focus on telling a story, not reciting facts
- Remember: You've built something real. You have every right to be confident.
